# Daily Brief Template

```
[AI Security Brief] YYYY-MM-DD

1. HEADLINE — one-sentence summary, punchy and clear (source, date)
2. HEADLINE — one-sentence summary, punchy and clear (source, date)
3. HEADLINE — one-sentence summary, punchy and clear (source, date)

Insight: one or two sentences — sharp, opinionated, connects the dots.
```

## Tone Guide

- Each headline should hook the reader mid-scroll. Lead with the interesting part.
- Summaries: one sentence, plain language. If your grandma can't follow it, simplify.
- Insight: this is your voice. Be opinionated. A good analogy or dry observation goes a long way.
- Avoid: "groundbreaking", "game-changing", "unprecedented". If it's actually big, the facts speak for themselves.

## Instructions

- Replace `YYYY-MM-DD` with today's date.
- Cite the original source and publication date in each item.
- The Insight section should leave readers thinking, not just nodding.
